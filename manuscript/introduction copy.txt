SYNOPSIS OF FORMER CHAPTERS.

Mr. J. E. Barrington a brilliant and wealthy young lawyer and politician of one of the great middle western states is duped and sold out by the professional politicians in his campaign for congress, and chagrined and embittered against his country and the entire human race be converts all of his property into gold and going to New York purchases a vessel with which be sails down into the Antarctic regions with the intention of devoting his life and means to polar explorations or finding some isolated country beyond the pale of civilisation and out of the track of sea travel where he will be lost to the world.
